Teacher's Appreciation Week

This is Teacher Appreciation Week and the school has a schedule of events to give thanks to the teachers and staff of Laravel Mail. Monday was lunch, Tuesday is Snow Cones, Wednesday is BBQ and homemade ice cream, Thursday is Breakfast and a Coffee Bar and Friday will be the best of all - Prizes and sweets!  The Laravel Mail teachers and staff would like to thank the Laravel Mail County 4-H Club for providing Ice Cream to Laravel Mail teachers Monday!
